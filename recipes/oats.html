Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are convenient and hearty breakfast option. Customize this dish with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ milk of your choice
  • 2 tbsp. honey or sweetener of choice
  • toppings of choice (this recipe uses strawberries and granola)

Steps

  1. Pour oats into a mason jar or meal prep container
  2. Add milk and sweetener of choice
  3. Mix well and place in fridge for several hours or overnight
  4. Finish with toppings of choice!
